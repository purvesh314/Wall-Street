R Rules

  • Each participant will be provided with a unique Login ID and password to log on the Wall Street Webite and start playing. He/she is expected to buy the shares using this cash amount.

  • As soon as the market opens at 10:00 AM on the first day (21 st Sep), every participant will be allowed to login into his account.

  • The market will be up from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on 21 st , 22 nd & 23 rd September 2018. This period is defined as “day” henceforth in the document.

  • Participant has to buy stocks of at least 10 different companies, in order to get on the leaderboard.

  • Any buy bid or sell ask can be made within a margin of ±10% of the current share price. For ex, if the current share price of a share is Rs.100/-, a buy or sell bid can be made from any range between Rs. 90- Rs. 110/-

  • Maximum number of shares in one bid is 200

  • A transaction fee of 0.5% of total transaction cost will be charged from both the seller and the buyer for every successful transaction between the two.

  • When a user places a buy bid for the shares of company A, the amount he’s bidding for will be immediately cut from his account along with the relevant transaction fee of 0.5%. For ex., if the buyer buys 50 shares for Rs. 106 each share, the cost will be Rs.5300/-. Adding the 0.5% transaction charge the final cost that’ll be cut from the buyer’s account will be 1.05*5300= Rs.5565/- This amount will be immediately deducted, and the user won’t be able to access this amount.

  • The Ranking of the user is decided using a “Valuation Formula”. Valuation of the user will be 60% of his total volume cost in Shares acquired + 40% of the cash balance in his account. This total will be termed as the valuation amount.

  • Only Integral Bids allowed. Decimal values will be invalid.

  • Participants can be disqualified for MALPRACTICES

  • News regarding the listed companies would be displayed every hour.

  • Portfolios of all listed companies have been provided for the participants convenience

  • Unexecuted bid would be deleted after 1 hour. Transaction fee would be refunded in case of deleted bids.

  • Trading bonuses will be given to participants according to the amount traded.

  • Participants can also manually revoke the bids (From Day 2). Brokerage fees would be charged for revoking BUYING bids. No fees would be charged on revoking selling bids.
